About

Viral fever is caused by viral infections leading to high temperature and fatigue.

WHO Data

WHO emphasizes hygiene and vaccination as preventive measures against viral infections.

Treatment

  • Paracetamol (doctor advised)
  • Hydration
  • Rest

When to Consult a Doctor

  • Fever above 102°F
  • Severe weakness
  • Rashes or breathing problems
