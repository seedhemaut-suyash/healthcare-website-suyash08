🦠 COVID-19

About

COVID-19 is a contagious respiratory illness caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. It spreads mainly through respiratory droplets and close contact. It can affect lungs, heart, brain and other organs.

WHO Data

According to WHO, COVID-19 caused over 770 million confirmed cases globally and more than 7 million deaths. Vaccination significantly reduces severe illness and death.

Symptoms

  • Fever
  • Dry cough
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Breathing difficulty
  • Fatigue

Prevention

  • Vaccination
  • Mask in crowded areas
  • Hand hygiene
  • Proper ventilation

Treatment

  • Isolation
  • Rest and hydration
  • Antiviral medicines (doctor prescribed)
  • Oxygen therapy in severe cases

When to Consult Doctor

  • Difficulty breathing
  • Persistent chest pain
  • Oxygen level below 94%
