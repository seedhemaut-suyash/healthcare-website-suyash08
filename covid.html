About

COVID-19 is caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus affecting the respiratory system.

WHO Data

According to WHO, COVID-19 spreads mainly through respiratory droplets. Vaccination significantly reduces severe illness and death.

How to Cure / Treatment

  • Isolation
  • Rest and hydration
  • Antiviral medicines (as prescribed)
  • Oxygen support in severe cases

When to Consult a Doctor

  • Difficulty breathing
  • Persistent chest pain
  • High fever for more than 3 days
